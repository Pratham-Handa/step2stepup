Our Mission

At Step2StepUp, we believe that entrepreneurship is a proven pathway to economic freedom. We coach, fund, and network young adults so they can build sustainable ventures and uplift their communities in return.

Our Vision

A future where every young adult has the tools, confidence, and networks to create their own prosperity.

Why We Exist

Traditional education often lacks practical entrepreneurial training. We bridge that gap, helping untapped potential flourish.

What We Do

Our flagship program combines rigorous business training, dedicated mentorship, and investor access to launch viable enterprises.

Entrepreneurial Training

Mentorship & Coaching

One-on-one pairing with experienced entrepreneurs and domain experts to guide growth.

Meet Our Founder

Vasu (Vasundhara) Pawar

Founder & CEO, Step2StepUp, Inc.

Vasu Pawar is the visionary Founder and CEO of Step2StepUp, Inc., a U.S.-based non-profit [501(c)(3)] dedicated to empowering young adults through entrepreneurship, mentorship, and financial literacy. With over 35 years of leadership experience across finance, education, and business operations, Vasu brings a rare blend of corporate acumen and social purpose.

Her vision for Step2StepUp extends globally with ongoing initiatives in the U.S., Australia, New Zealand, Fiji, Uganda, and India, aiming to build a generation of young entrepreneurs who succeed, sustain, and give back.
